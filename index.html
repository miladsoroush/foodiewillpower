Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on our apps for iPhone, Android on any devices.

Information we collect

Device authentication is created anonymously, without email addresses or passwords, and only requires a participation code. We store information about how you use the app and survey data in the app.

Technical basics

Our app may also store basic technical information in temporary memory or logs, for example, whether the device is Android or iOS. The app does not collect IP addresses.

Firebase Services

Foodie Willpower stores some of your data using Firebase Authentication and Storage services.

Information usage

We do not collect or share personal information with outside parties. We may share anonymous, aggregate statistics with outside parties, for example, presenting in a conference or publication.

We may disclose the open-ended responses in response to our University of Waterloo ethics department inquiries regarding in case there is a concern regarding participants’ health, or under subpoenas, court orders, or other legal requirements; to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud or abuse, violations of our policies; or to protect our rights and property.

Security

We implement a variety of security measures to help keep your information secure such as keeping the stored data securely and using Firebase Authentication services.

Accessing, changing, or deleting information

You may delete your account by deleting your iOS app. We may delete your information at any time and for any reason, such as technical needs, legal concerns, abuse prevention, removal of idle accounts, data loss, or any other reason.

Privacy Protection Compliance

We comply with the privacy protection policy presented to you in the information letter form. We protect your privacy according to the terms of the letter.

Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

The app participation code is provided only for adults. We never collect or maintain information from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our app is structured to attract anyone under 13.

International Transfers of Information

Information may be processed, stored, and used outside of the country in which you are located. Data privacy laws vary across jurisdictions, and different laws may be applicable to your data depending on where it is processed, stored, or used.

Your Consent

By using apps, you consent to our privacy policy.

Contacting Us

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, you may email uwaterloo.studies@gmail.com.
